Interactive Excel Dashboard

The analysis of coffee sales data reveals key insights into consumer preferences, market trends, and potential growth opportunities. By examining various metrics such as time period, roast type name, size, and loyalty, patterns and correlations can be identified to inform strategic decision-making. Some key insights found:

Identify peak sales periods: Overall, 2021 has the highest sales; Arabica beans sold well in September and Excelsa sold well in November. This trend does not apply to different time period; for instance, Arabica sold well in Feb 2020. This can be useful information to Inventory Management to predict and plan marketing strategies.

Seasonal trends: Sales seems to be high in February, June and October, promotional activities and new product launching can be planned for the upcoming years.

Popular roast types: Customers are shifting their preference from Excelsa to Arabica coffee beans, allowing for targeted marketing and inventory management.

Variation in sales by roast type: Light is the best seller in terms of roast type, customers are consuming more caffeine nowadays, which can inform product development and purchasing decisions.

Preferred coffee sizes: Customers tend to purchase 2.5Kg size more frequently, enabling optimization of product offerings and pricing strategies.

Customer loyalty trends: Almost half of the customers purchased coffee beans with the influence of loyalty card, there's opportunities for customer retention, loyalty program enhancements, prioritize marketing efforts and customer relationship management strategies.

This end-to-end Excel project includes data collection, transformation, pivot table and charts to
build a dynamic interactive coffee sales dashboard. Please click on See Interactive Dashboard for full access.

London Bike Rides

This Tableau visualization project was generated by programatically collecting, exploring,
evaluating, and manipulating data using panda library in Python.This offers insights into rider behavior, patterns, and trends. It analyzes metrics like ride frequency, temperature, weather, hours of time and wind speed. Key findings include:

Usage Patterns: Summer month between July to September is when bike rides are more popular, which can inform decisions related to bike availability, maintenance schedules, and staffing.

Temperature vs. Speed: Users can explore the temperature and wind speed for the ride durations. This information helps understand typical ride durations, as well as variations based on factors such as hours of time and weather. Riders typically pick scattered cloud days to ride in and the typical temperature is between 8 to 18 degrees.

King County, Washington House Sales

This Tableau visualization project was generated by utilizing calender widget,
as main filter, a line chart, a map, histogram and heat map to showcase overview of the house prices based on conditions such as years built and squarefoot living.

Daily Average House Sales Price: The line chart reveals how average house prices have evolved over time. Average price drastically rised during around middle and at the end of the month.

Map: The map visualization likely highlights geographical variations in house prices, allowing users to identify regions with higher or lower property values. Insights could include understanding the influence of location, neighborhood characteristics, and amenities on housing prices.

Distribution of House Prices: The histogram provide insights into the distribution of house prices within specific ranges, revealing the concentration of properties within certain price brackets and outliers at the higher or lower ends of the spectrum. The most common house prices is between 250K and 450K within King County, Washington.

Distribution of Bedrooms/Bathrooms: The histogram could help identify the most common usage of bedrooms and bathrooms; 3 for bedrooms and 2-3 bathrooms.

View vs Condition Heatmap: The heat map provides a visual representation of how data points are distributed geographically. By color-coding areas based on the density of data points, it allows for quick identification of house views vs. conditions. The better the view of the house, the better the condition is.
